FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What pairs does SweepSignal cover?

28 instruments including all major pairs (EUR/USD, GBP/USD, USD/JPY, etc.), popular crosses (EUR/GBP, GBP/JPY, AUD/NZD, etc.), plus XAU/USD (Gold) and NAS100.

How is the signal grade calculated?

Each signal is scored 0-100 based on five factors: level quality (PDH/PDL scores highest), displacement strength, FVG presence, higher timeframe alignment, and risk-to-reward ratio. Scores of 90+ = A+, 80-89 = A, 70-79 = B.

How many alerts will I receive?

It depends on market conditions. The system only alerts on B grade and above, and each signal has a 60-minute cooldown to prevent spam. Expect anywhere from 0-10 alerts on a typical trading day.
